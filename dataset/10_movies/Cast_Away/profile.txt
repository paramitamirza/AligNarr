Cast Away
The mood of Cast Away is gloomy, contemplative, and captivating.
The plot centers around isolation, the human spirit, and a "fish out of water".
It is a drama and adventure movie.
Stylistically, Cast Away has a Hollywood tone.
In approach, it is serious and realistic.
The pacing is slow.
Cast Away takes place, at least partly, on an island.
It happens in the 1990s.
The movie is known for being a modern classic, a blockbuster, and critically acclaimed.
Cast Away is especially suggested for a date night and a family outing.
