Profile of Cars 2

The mood of Cars 2 is stylized and humorous. The plot centers around a car chase, undercover characters, and buddies. It is a comedy, animation, and adventure movie. Stylistically, Cars 2 has a Hollywood tone. In approach, it is fantastical. It happens in contemporary times. Visually, Cars 2 is 3D and is computer animated. The movie is known for being a blockbuster. It is especially suggested for a family outing.

Racecar Lightning McQueen (Owen Wilson) and his tow-truck buddy, Mater (Larry the Cable Guy), have had their share of adventures together but nothing like what they experience when they head overseas for the first-ever World Grand Prix.
