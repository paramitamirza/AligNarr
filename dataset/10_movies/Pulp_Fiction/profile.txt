Pulp Fiction
Pulp Fiction can be described as clever, stylized, and rough.
The plot revolves around a violence spree, criminal heroes, and dishonesty.
Its comic aspect comes from dark humor and irreverent humor.
Pulp Fiction's main genres are independent, crime, and music-oriented.
In terms of style, it is postmodernist, features an all-star cast, and is nonlinear.
In approach, it is realistic.
Pulp Fiction is set, at least in part, in an urban environment.
It is located in Los Angeles.
It takes place in the 1990s.
The soundtrack of Pulp Fiction is pop, rock, and soul.
The movie has received attention for being groundbreaking, original, and a cult favorite.
It is well suited for a boys' night.
Note that Pulp Fiction involves strong sexual content, drugs/alcohol, and profanity.
