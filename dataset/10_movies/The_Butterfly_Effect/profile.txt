The Butterfly Effect
The Butterfly Effect can be described as suspenseful, mind bending, and stylized.
The plot revolves around changing the past or future, time travel, and being haunted by the past.
The main genres are thriller and sci-fi.
In terms of style, The Butterfly Effect is nonlinear and involves twists and turns.
In approach, it is fantastical and serious.
It is located in the USA.
The Butterfly Effect takes place in contemporary times.
Note that it involves drugs/alcohol, sexual content, and profanity.
