Shrek
The mood of Shrek is witty, feel good, and clever.
The plot centers around reluctant partners, an odd couple, and a daring rescue.
It is a comedy, animation, and adventure movie.
Stylistically, Shrek is fairytale-like, has a Hollywood tone, and features an all-star cast.
In approach, it is not serious and fantastical.
Visually, it is computer animated.
The musical score of Shrek is pop.
It is based on a book.
The movie is known for being a modern classic, an Oscar winner, and a blockbuster.
Shrek is especially suggested for a date night and a family outing.
